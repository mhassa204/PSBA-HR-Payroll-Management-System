# PSBA HR Management System - User Manual

## Part 4: Leave Management

---

## Overview

The Leave Management module allows employees to apply for leaves, track leave balances, and manage the leave approval process. This module supports multiple leave types, leave banks, and workflow-based approvals.

**Key Features:**

- Apply for leave online
- View leave balances
- Multi-stage leave approval workflow
- Leave bank management
- Leave history and reports

**Who Can Use This Module:**

- All employees (to apply for leave)
- Approvers (Directors, HoDs, Management)
- HR Staff (to manage leave banks)

---

## Understanding Leave Management

### Leave Bank

A Leave Bank is a period during which leave allocations are defined (e.g., a fiscal year). Each bank has:

- Start and end dates
- Leave types available
- Default allocations per type
- Individual allocations for employees

### Leave Types

Different types of leave include:

- **Annual Leave:** Regular vacation time
- **Sick Leave:** Medical leave
- **Casual Leave:** Short personal leaves
- **Earned Leave:** Accrued over time
- **Other:** Custom leave types as defined

### Leave Balance

Your leave balance shows:

- Total allocated days
- Days already taken
- Remaining balance available

## Applying for Leave

### Accessing Leave Application

1. From the main menu, click **"Leave"** or navigate to **Attendance > Apply Leave**
2. The system displays the leave application form
3. View your current leave balance summary

### Filling Out the Leave Application

#### Step 1: Basic Information

1. **Select Leave Date**

   - Click on the date field
   - Choose the date from calendar
   - You can select multiple consecutive days

2. **Select Leave Type**

   - Click the dropdown menu
   - Choose appropriate leave type:
     - Annual Leave
     - Sick Leave
     - Casual Leave
     - Earned Leave
     - Other (if applicable)

3. **Custom Leave Type** (if you selected "Other")
   - Enter description of leave type
   - Provide additional details

#### Step 2: Duty Hours (Optional)

If your leave is for part of the day:

1. **Duty Time From**

   - Enter start time (HH:mm format)
   - Example: 09:00

2. **Duty Time To**
   - Enter end time (HH:mm format)
   - Example: 14:00

#### Step 3: Backup Employee

Assign someone to handle your duties:

1. **Select Backup Employee**

   - Search or select from dropdown
   - Choose colleague who can cover

2. **Backup Duty Hours** (Optional)
   - Specify time when backup is needed
   - From: Enter start time
   - To: Enter end time

#### Step 4: Remarks

Enter any additional information:

- Reason for leave
- Important notes
- Contact information during leave
- Other relevant details

#### Step 5: Supporting Documents (Optional)

Upload supporting documents if required:

- Medical certificate for sick leave
- Travel documents
- Other supporting files

1. Click **"Upload Document"**
2. Select file from computer
3. Document will be attached to application

#### Step 6: Submit Application

1. Review all information
2. Click **"Submit"** or **"Apply for Leave"** button
3. System will create leave request
4. You'll see confirmation message
5. Application status will be "Pending"

## Leave Approval Process

### Understanding the Workflow

Leave applications go through an approval workflow:

1. **Submitted:** You submit the application
2. **Recommendation Stage:** First approver reviews
3. **Allow Stage:** Second approver reviews
4. **Approval Stage:** Final approval granted
5. **Approved:** Leave is authorized

### Who Can Approve

Approvers depend on:

- Organization hierarchy
- Employee's reporting officer
- Department structure
- System configuration

Common approvers:

- **Immediate Supervisor:** Direct manager
- **Department Head (HoD):** Head of Department
- **Director/Management:** Senior management
- **HR/Establishment:** HR department

## Approving Leave Requests

### Accessing Approval Queue

1. From Attendance menu, click **"Leave Approvals"**
2. View list of pending leave requests

### Viewing Approval Requests

The list shows:

- Employee name
- Leave date
- Leave type
- Status
- Submission date

### Reviewing a Leave Request

1. Click on a leave request
2. View detailed information:
   - Employee details
   - Leave dates and type
   - Backup employee
   - Remarks
   - Supporting documents
   - Current status

### Making an Approval Decision

#### To Recommend

1. Review leave details
2. Ensure it's valid and within policy
3. Click **"Recommend"** button
4. Optionally add comments
5. Submit

#### To Allow

1. Review leave and previous recommendations
2. Click **"Allow"** button
3. Add comments if needed
4. Submit

#### To Approve

1. Review complete information
2. Verify all approvals are complete
3. Click **"Approve"** button
4. Leave is now approved
5. Employee will be notified

#### To Reject

1. Review leave request
2. If invalid or against policy, click **"Reject"**
3. Enter rejection reason (required)
4. Submit rejection

### Important Notes on Approvals

- **Sequential Approval:** Approvals must follow the sequence
- **Comments:** Always provide clear comments
- **Documents:** Review supporting documents
- **Balance:** Verify employee has available balance
- **Policy Compliance:** Ensure leave complies with organizational policy

## Leave Bank Management

**Note:** Only HR Staff can manage leave banks.

### Accessing Leave Bank

1. Navigate to Attendance > Leave Bank
2. View existing leave banks

### Creating a Leave Bank

1. Click **"Create Leave Bank"**
2. Enter information:
   - **Title:** Name of leave bank (e.g., "Fiscal Year 2024")
   - **Period Start:** First day of period
   - **Period End:** Last day of period
3. Click **"Save"**

### Setting Default Allocations

1. Open leave bank
2. Go to defaults section
3. Set default days per leave type:
   - Annual Leave: 15 days
   - Sick Leave: 10 days
   - Casual Leave: 5 days
   - Etc.
4. Save defaults

### Allocating Leave to Employees

1. Open leave bank
2. Click **"Allocate to Employees"**
3. Select employee
4. Set allocations per leave type
5. Save allocations

## Common Tasks

### Task: Apply for One Day Leave

1. Go to Apply Leave
2. Select single date
3. Choose leave type
4. Enter remarks
5. Submit

### Task: Apply for Multiple Days Leave

1. Go to Apply Leave
2. Select first date
3. Hold Shift and select last date
4. All dates will be selected
5. Complete application
6. Submit

### Task: Apply for Half Day Leave

1. Go to Apply Leave
2. Select date
3. Set duty time from/to
4. Complete application
5. Submit

1. Go to My Leaves
2. Find pending leave
3. Click **"Cancel"** or **"Withdraw"**
4. Confirm cancellation

### Task: Check Remaining Leave Balance

1. Go to Apply Leave
2. View balance summary at top
3. See remaining days by type

2. **Check Balance:** Verify available balance before applying

### For Approvers

1. **Timely Action:** Approve/reject promptly

3. **Policy Compliance:** Ensure compliance with rules
4. **Clear Comments:** Provide constructive feedback

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Apply for Leave

- Check your leave balance
- Verify dates are valid
- Ensure no conflicting leaves
- Contact HR if issue persists

### Leave Not Showing in List

- Check filters applied
- Verify date range
- Refresh the page
- Check if leave was deleted

### Cannot Approve Leave

- Verify you have approval permissions
- Check if leave is in correct stage
- Ensure you are authorized approver
- Contact system administrator

### Leave Balance Incorrect

- Report to HR department
- Provide details of discrepancy
- HR will investigate and correct

## Important Policies

### Leave Allocation

- Standard allocations defined per leave bank
- Different for different employee categories
- Check with HR for specific allocations

### Leave Carry Forward

- Some leaves may be carried forward
- Others may expire at year end
- Check policy with HR department

### Notice Period

- Advance notice required for planned leaves
- Emergency leaves may have different rules
- Check organizational policy

### Medical Leave

- Medical certificate required
- May have limits on consecutive days
- Follow organizational procedures
